Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A standard website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while branding projects may take 3-4 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your payment structure?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, I can arrange milestone-based payments. All payment terms are clearly outlined in our contract.

Do you offer maintenance services after project completion?

Yes, I offer various maintenance packages to ensure your website or digital assets remain up-to-date and secure. These can be arranged on a monthly or quarterly basis, depending on your needs.

How do we get started working together?

Simply contact me through the contact form, email, or phone to schedule an initial consultation. During this meeting, we'll discuss your goals, requirements, and expectations to determine if we're a good fit for working together.
